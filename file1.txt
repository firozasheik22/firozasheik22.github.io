WHERE THERE IS A WILL, THERE IS A WAY.
This proverb has a lot of imporatance.
It explains us the importance of will power to find a way to perform any task.
This will power makes people more determined and focused to achieve their goal.
And if we work continuously to achieve your goals, you can surely find a way.
So, this proverb, Where there is a will, there is a way teaches us to be quite focused and hard working.
